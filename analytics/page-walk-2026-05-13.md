## Updated Verdict: Is the report worth $14.99?

**Yes** — but only if the customer gets to see it. The gap isn't the report's quality (it's excellent). The gap is that customers who hit the free version don't have a clear path to upgrade. The sample report is also showing a stale 98/100 score from Nov 2025 that looks less trustworthy the older it gets.

**Top 3 surprises:**
1. **No /cancel page** — Stripe checkout cancel redirect has no landing page. Customers cancel and see something raw.
2. **Sample report date is 6 months stale** — The static sample report (`/sample-report`) was generated Nov 2025 with a 98/100 score. This is the biggest presentation risk.
3. **/recommend duplicates homepage** — The URL `/recommend` has identical content to the homepage with the same form. No obvious reason for both to exist.
