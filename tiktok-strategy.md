# SEO CheckSite — TikTok Strategy

## Content Pillars (matching X/Twitter strategy)
| Pillar | % | Examples |
|--------|---|---------|
| SEO Tips & How-Tos | 40% | Audit findings, fix guides, tool demos |
| Building in Public | 25% | Growth stats, new features, dev updates |
| Industry Commentary | 20% | Google updates, AI trends, SEO news |
| Engagement/Humor | 15% | SEO memes, replies, community interaction |

## Posting Schedule
- **Max 1-2 videos per day** — Quality over quantity
- **Best times:** 7-9am, 12-1pm, 7-9pm ET
- **Min 60 min between actions** — Avoid rate limiting

## Engagement Rules
- Like 10-15 SEO/tech accounts per session
- Follow relevant accounts (SEO tools, agencies, educators)
- Comment genuine insights (no generic "Great post!")
- Engage with competitors' content constructively

## Content Pipeline
1. Research trending SEO topics via web_fetch
2. Generate video scripts (from existing batch or new)
3. Create videos using ffmpeg + Pillow
4. Upload via tiktok-uploader
5. Track in memory/social-log.json
6. Engage with relevant accounts hourly

## Video Templates
1. **Audit Result Card** — Screenshot-style with findings
2. **Quick Tip** — Single SEO tip, 15-20 seconds
3. **Comparison** — Before/after or free vs paid tool
4. **Story** — Building process, dev update
