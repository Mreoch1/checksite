# SEO CheckSite — TikTok Production Standard

## Core Requirements
- Vertical (9:16), 1080x1920
- Duration: 15-45 seconds
- Fast-paced, strong hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Bright, vibrant, high contrast visuals
- Seamless transitions

## Structure (from production prompt)
1. **Hook (0-3s):** Bold text + motion, curiosity spark
2. **Main (3-30s):** Multiple short slides (2-5s each), dynamic visuals, kinetic typography
3. **Voiceover:** Natural, energetic, synced with visuals
4. **Audio:** Trending background + layered sound effects
5. **Visual Style:** Bright palette, zooms/pans, modern aesthetic
6. **Captions:** Always on-screen, large, bold, animated
7. **Ending (last 3-5s):** Strong payoff, CTA, loop-friendly

## Advanced Enhancements
- Pattern interrupts every 2-4 seconds
- Motion graphics overlays
- Emotional pacing shifts
- Loop optimization

## Tech Stack
- Pillow (Python) — Card/prop creation
- ffmpeg — Video assembly + transitions
- Generated audio — Background music + effects
- Browser automation — TikTok upload
